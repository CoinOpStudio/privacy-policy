Privacy Policy - Chroma Strata

Last updated: July 2026

CoinOp Studio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile game Chroma Strata.

1. Information We Collect

Chroma Strata does not require registration, does not create user accounts, and does not directly collect or store personal information.

The application may automatically collect certain information through third-party services used in the app, such as advertising services.

2. Advertising

Chroma Strata uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect information such as device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data to provide and measure advertisements.

For more information about how Google handles data, please visit:

Google Privacy Policy

3. Internet Connection

The application uses an internet connection to load advertisements and provide related services. If an internet connection is unavailable, advertisements may not be displayed.

4. In-App Purchases

Chroma Strata may offer an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. CoinOp Studio does not access or store payment information.

5. Permissions

The application may use device vibration functionality to provide gameplay feedback. No other sensitive device permissions are requested.

6. Data Sharing

CoinOp Studio does not sell, trade, or directly share personal information. Some information may be processed by third-party services required for advertising and payment functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Chroma Strata is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.
